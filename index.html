About

I’m an Iranian-born filmmaker, director, cinematographer, and editor based in the Bay Area.

I work with brands, founders, and cultural organizations to turn complex ideas into cinematic stories that connect with audiences and perform across digital platforms.

With over a decade of experience in documentary filmmaking and digital media, my work has been featured on PBS, Amazon Prime Video, VICE, CBS Bay Area, Hulu, and The San Francisco Standard.

I’ve collaborated with organizations including Burning Man, Sequoia Capital, Alpine Investors, and DFINITY, leading projects from concept through production, editing, and delivery.

In 2025, I grew my Instagram audience from 2,000 to over 95,000 followers by applying documentary storytelling principles to short-form video — combining drone cinematography, narrative pacing, and distribution strategy.

Work With Me

I collaborate with brands, startups, founders, artists, and cultural organizations on video production, storytelling strategy, and content creation.

Whether it’s a short-form campaign, documentary piece, branded content, or a cinematic social strategy, I handle the full process from concept to final delivery.
